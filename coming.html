Automation

Here at Los Santos Life Australia, we have a lot of different jobs to offer. Some of which, can be AFK'able in a sense.
For instance, Fishing, Lumbering, Fueling and Tailoring typically have 3-4 steps invloved, from collection, to finishing touches to selling.
These can be completed in an AFK'able method where a togglable macro can be activated on your eligable keyboard/mouse or AHK and can then complete the steps with minimal human requirements. Unfortunately, withouth scripts, we're unable to leave the GTA window, so you can't use your computer while this method is being utilised.
If set up correctly, the only thing you'll need to return to complete, is relocating one each step has been complete - to go to the next location. Each step will require a different macro generally.
